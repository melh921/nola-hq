Everything you’d otherwise have to dig out of the registration email.

What’s the dress code?
Cocktail attire or better. New Orleans in June is hot and humid — expect 85°F+. You’ll be outside briefly at wristband pickup so factor that in.
Where do I get my wristband?
Check-in opens at 4:00 pm at the Hilton New Orleans Riverside. You need it to enter the event — don’t leave the hotel without it.
What time does it start?
Doors open 6:00 pm, runs until midnight. Most people aim to arrive 6:00–6:30 pm.
Can I use staff travel for flights?
No — flights must go through the event registration portal via Concur. Staff travel bookings won’t be reimbursed. Log back in with confirmation number WPNG9JXFXNF to book.
Hotel address?
Hilton New Orleans Riverside · 2 Poydras St · New Orleans, LA 70130. Right on the Mississippi River, close to the French Quarter.
How do I save this to my phone home screen?
iPhone: Tap the Share button in Safari, then “Add to Home Screen.”

Android: Tap the three-dot menu in Chrome, then “Add to Home screen.”

It will appear as an icon and open full-screen like an app — no download required.
Who built this app?
The FCM Technology Area AI Initiative Taskforce — Melanie H, Marissa, and August. Built using AI tools as a live demonstration of what the program enables. Find us at the ball if you want to know how it was made.
Questions or problems?
Event team: FCTGevents@us.fcm.travel · Confirmation number: WPNG9JXFXNF
